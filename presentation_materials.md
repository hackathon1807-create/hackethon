## Slide 2: The Problem
**Headline:** The Era of Synthetic Exploitation
**Talking Points:**
- **Rampant Deepfakes:** Exploitative synthetic media (revenge porn, political manipulation, financial scams) has become terrifyingly easy to generate.
- **Voice Spoofing:** Audio cloning enables advanced social engineering that bypasses traditional biometric security.
- **The Privacy Dilemma:** Existing cloud-based detection APIs require victims to upload sensitive, potentially compromising media to third-party servers, revictimizing them and breaking chain-of-custody.

## Slide 3: The Solution (Meipporul AI)
**Headline:** Empowering Victims with the Ultimate Unified Dashboard
**Talking Points:**
- **Zero-Cloud Architecture:** Absolute privacy. The entire pipeline (Detection Heuristics + LLM + OSINT) runs entirely on the user's local hardware. No data ever leaves the machine.
- **The 3-Phase Resolution Pipeline:**
  - **Phase 1: Detection:** Upload a file or paste a URL to instantly calculate deepfake probability.
  - **Phase 2: Trace (OSINT):** Instantly trace the IP, Registrar, and physical geolocation of the server hosting the deepfake.
  - **Phase 3: Takedown:** Generate actionable legal reports and instantly access direct global DMCA takedown queues.
